# Sleep Poses

Sleep poses can be selected from **Sleep Poses** in the top menu.

:::note[Note]

Sleep Poses is a system that **replaces** VRChat's standard "crouch/prone" behavior. Simply selecting a pose in the menu does not activate it. After selecting a pose, it activates by **physically lowering your HMD** (crouching or going prone) or by **dragging the play space with OVR Advanced Settings Spacemover**.

:::

---

- Prone (face down)
- Supine (face up)
- Side-lying
- On back / sitting on floor
- **Pose Transition Lock** — Locks the pose so it does not change (`VRCLockPose`)
- **Foot Anchor** — Fixes the foot position to the floor (`VRCFootAnchor`)

:::note[Note]

Turning on **Pose Transition Lock** while in a sleep pose prevents the pose from being released by movement or actions.

:::
