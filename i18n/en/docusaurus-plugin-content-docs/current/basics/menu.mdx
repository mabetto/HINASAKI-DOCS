## FX Controls

By toggling the gesture change buttons for each hand, you can access additional hand gestures — such as cat, heart, finger heart, gun, and phone — in addition to the standard 8 poses. Left and right can be configured independently, allowing you to use different gestures on each hand simultaneously.

## Sleep Poses

This system replaces VRChat's standard "crouch/prone" behavior. After selecting a pose in the menu, it activates by **physically lowering your HMD** or by **dragging the play space with OVR Advanced Settings Spacemover**.
